Symptoms :

Most common Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Tiredness
    •

    Less common symptoms

  • Aches and pains
  • Sore throat
  • Diarrhoea
  • Conjunctivitis
  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • A rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers   or toes
    •

    Severe Symptoms

  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Loss of speech or movement
    •

    Precautions :

    Clean hands often

    Use soap and water, or alcohol-based sanitizer/hand rubs to clean your hands regularly

    Maintain social distancing

    It is important to keep a physical distance of minimum 2m whenever going out of home.

    Wear a mask

    Always wear a mask when around people

    Don't touch your face

    Avoid touching your eyes, mouth or nose to avoid risk of getting infected.

    Cover your mouth while sneezing or coughing

    Always cover your mouth with a handkerchief or your elbow when sneezing or coughing around people.

    Stay Home

    Quarantine yourself if feeling unwell.

    Medical attention if necessary

    Seek medical attention if you show any symptoms. Call and ask your doctor before visiting them.

    GET vaccinated, Stay safe!

    Get vaccinated if you are eleigible and keep yourself and your family safe.
